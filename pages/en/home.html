Online Municipal Council

This service allows you to perform the tasks performed by the Municipal Council very quickly and easily. Through this service, you can effectively, quickly and easily make payment for assessment payments, shop rent payments, make reservations for auditoriums, community halls, crematoriums, street bowsers, etc., and download all applications online. You can also view the information of the Municipal Council and details of all services instantly. Residents of the area can access this service from anywhere in the world. Payments can be made using any bank card and when you make a payment, you will be directed to the online payment gateway of the Republic of Sri Lanka, so it can be done more reliably. Through this system, about 90% of the entire process carried out by the Pradeshiya Sabha can be carried out very quickly and efficiently without having to visit the Municipal Council, thus providing superior service to the residents of the area as well as anyone who needs to avail these services from the Municipal Council.

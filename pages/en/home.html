Online Municipal Council

Welcome to the Online Wattala - Mabola Municipal Council Service. Through this service, you can quickly and easily accomplish tasks provided by the Municipal Council. You can efficiently pay property taxes, shop rentals, reserve the auditorium, community halls, crematorium, gully bowser, etc., and download all application forms through the internet. You can also instantly view information about all services provided by the Municipal Council. Residents can access this service from anywhere in the world. Payments can be made using any bank card, and when making payments, you will be directed through the Sri Lankan government's online payment gateway, making it very reliable. Through this system, about 90% of the Municipal Council's entire process can be completed quickly and efficiently without visiting the council, providing a superior service to residents and anyone requiring these services from the Municipal Council.
